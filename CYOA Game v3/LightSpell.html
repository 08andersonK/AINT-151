Let there be LIGHT!!!

Before you go stepping into the dark unknown, you feel a hand grab your shoulder. Turning to face the one who dared lay a hand on you without permission, you see it as none other than the Wizard you hired.

“Before you go getting lost in the dark… I suggest we create some light… Better to be able to see where we are lost, than to be lost and unable to see… or something like that” The Elf says with a hint of sarcasm, still… You agree, and ask if he has any means to create light.

Looking down the dark descent, he begins to chant and, with a flick of his staff, balls of magical energy begin floating around him.

With your now mobile light source, you can safely descend into the darkness, avoiding some broken stairs that could have caused a nasty fall...
