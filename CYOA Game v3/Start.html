Intro

The land of Nerva is an old one. It’s history is filled to the brim with constant conflict with monsters from both inside and out. It's currently ruled over by a high council of nobles and in charge of them are the Council of three; Three Powerful and influential people that have the final say in major actions the realm takes. Due to the constant danger the land has been under, there are parts of its history that have been lost to the realms of history. Due to this, some families and organisations have made it their goal to research these gaps in history and record them.

You are a highborn noble scholer, born into a family of nobles and charged with the documentation of ancient knowledge and artifacts. As you grew up you came to understand what your role in life would be, and that is to travel to strange and ancient locations and try to piece together the history of the world.

Today, you have finally received your first summons. Inside the letter that was delivered to your study, was the details of your task:

You are to travel from your home in the capital of Westlight, to a recent discovery in The Path, a sprawling wilderness that is the fastest way to cross from east to west. This recent discovery is a dungeon of unknown age or origin. It was discovered by a small group of adventurers who then decided to explore it… only one got out.

So it now comes to you to uphold your family name and delve into this ancient place and uncover it’s truth...
